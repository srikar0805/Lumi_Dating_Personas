About us.

Lumi was built by a three-person team at the University of New Haven for Web Development II, Spring 2026. We wanted to explore what social connection looks like when you stop flattening people into a single profile.

SS
Sai Srikar Kolli
Architect & Backend Lead

Built the Express server, REST API, JWT auth, matching algorithm, Hugging Face AI integration, Mongoose schemas, persona versioning, seed script, and deployment pipeline.

KK
Krishna Karra
Frontend Developer

Built the Angular component suite — PersonaForm, PersonaCard, PersonaList, Dashboard, MatchDashboard, Connect — along with reactive forms, routing, route guards, the auth interceptor, and overall styling.

DB
Dhanya Boyapally
Frontend + Data Layer

Built the D3 force-graph, ReportPanel, DriftTimeline, all Angular services and BehaviorSubject streams, AI service integration, and seed data QA.

Our mission

Most platforms give you one profile and one chance to make an impression. Lumi gives you selves — distinct personas for the different modes you actually live in. We match those selves to other people's selves, so connections form around something real.
